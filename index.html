Mission Statement

We believe that access to food is a human right


At the Bloor Food Bank, our aim is to help people in our community by supplementing their weekly amount of food. We strive to help as many people as possible by opening our bank each Wednesday, no matter the weather, and offering what we can. We accept any and all people in the community and that includes individuals, families and everyone in-between. We know that our food bank will not end hunger for our members, but we aim to be one of multiple charitable resources in the city and hope that together we may drastically reduce hunger and provide hope.

We accept and distribute the following items

Fresh Produce

Fruits and Vegetables in bulk from community grocers

Perishable Items

Grocery items with a limited shelf life

Non-Perishable Items

Grocery items with a very long shelf life

Frozen Goods

Any kind of frozen food item

Toiletries

Child and adult diapers, toilet paper, feminine hygiene products

Pet Food

Wet and dry food for cats and dogs
